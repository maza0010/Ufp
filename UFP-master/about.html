About Us

The Union for Progress (UFP) Is a non profit organization / charity that supports recent immigrants, mainly from African countries, in their transition to living in Canada by facilitating community and building initiatives. UFP wants to make the transition effortless for the newcomers in this great nation of canada. We also aim to raise funds for development projects in Africa, and more specifically in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and Uganda. Projects such as bringin renewable energy in africa, helping orphans, establishing more schools in africa and refugee settlements.

Objective

objective is to help people in needs of progress around the world as we can in different areas of their life.

Vision

The vision is supporting all people in needs by facilitating one on one connection within their communities, using these personal connections to bridge the gap between Canadians and their communities by raising funds for grassroots in all countries in need.

Mission

UFP will support people in needs from different countries around the world, in their life skills by facilitating world support and establishing connection between Canadians and their communities in order to make them feel that they have world support. we will also aim to raise funds for development projects in all countries in need of progress around the world
